			$s\in\mathcal O_X(D)(V)$ means \\
			$f_\alpha s$ is holomorphic on $V\cap U_\alpha$.\\[1mm]
			Equivalently, at each $p\in V$: \\
			$\ord_p(s)\ge -\ord_p(f_\alpha)$.\\[1mm]
			Winding form: \\
			$\displaystyle \ord_p(s)=\frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_{\gamma_p}\frac{ds}{s}$.
		};
